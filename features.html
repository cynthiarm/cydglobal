Entities

Hire employees remotely in different countries, quickly and easily, without having to worry about the paperwork, we take care of it.

Register and add employees quickly and easily to your entity, keep track of the employees related to your entity, grow or update your work team.

Payroll

Review salaries, incidences details and payroll payment in one click. Quickly and easily download or export your employee payroll list.

Quickly and easily download or export your employees' payroll list.

See the breakdown of employee payroll in detail. Select between one or several employees and obtain data on incidences and total payments of the employees.

Invoice

View the list of your invoices, details and more. Get the total amount to be paid in your currency at the current exchange rate of USD or MXN for the payment of the service and the payment of your employees.
